Aipreneurs Global Challenge 2025

Event Details and Guidelines

Challenge Overview

Aipreneurs Global Challenge 2025 invites innovators, students, and entrepreneurs from around the world to develop cutting-edge artificial intelligence solutions. This is your opportunity to create projects that address real-world problems, collaborate with peers, and gain recognition on a global stage.

Whether you're passionate about healthcare, education, sustainability, finance, or any other domain, we encourage you to harness the power of AI to create meaningful impact.

Eligibility Criteria

For Students

  • Currently enrolled in a recognized educational institution
  • Age 18 or older (with parental consent if under 18)
  • No prior professional experience required
  • Can participate individually or in teams of up to 5
  • Any field of study is welcome

For Professionals

  • Working professionals with AI/tech expertise
  • Can serve as mentors or jury members
  • Encouraged to support student teams
  • Opportunities for sponsorship and partnerships
  • Industry experts welcome to lead workshops

Project Guidelines

Submission Requirements

  • Project must use AI/ML technologies
  • Submit working code or prototype
  • Include detailed documentation
  • Provide demo video or presentation
  • Must be original work
  • Team member list and roles

Evaluation Criteria

  • Innovation and creativity (30%)
  • Technical implementation (25%)
  • Real-world impact and relevance (20%)
  • Code quality and documentation (15%)
  • Presentation and communication (10%)

Rules and Code of Conduct

General Rules

  • All work must be original and created during the challenge period
  • Teams can consist of 1-5 members
  • Participants can only be part of one team
  • No plagiarism or intellectual property violations
  • Respect confidentiality of judging criteria and other submissions
  • Follow all applicable laws and regulations
  • Maintain professionalism in all communications
  • No harassment or discriminatory behavior

Code of Conduct

  • Treat all participants with respect and dignity
  • Maintain ethical standards in your project
  • No offensive or discriminatory language
  • Protect personal data and privacy
  • Report violations to the organizing team
  • Participate in good faith and spirit of collaboration
